Instructions on how to draw a boundary
  1. Click or tap to place the first point of your boudary
  2. Place a second point on the boundary - you should see a line drawn between the points
  3. Keep adding points until the boundary of your site is fully defined
  4. When you have fully drawn your boundary double click or tap to mark your final point
  5. You can adjust the finished boundary by clicking and dragging the points
  6. You can add more points to a boundary by clicking or tapping on a line
  7. You can delete your drawn boundary and start again by clicking the 'Delete boundary' button
