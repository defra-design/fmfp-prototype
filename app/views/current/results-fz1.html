What flood zone 1 means

This location has a low probability of flooding from rivers and the sea. We do not have data for other sources of flooding.

When you need a flood risk assessment

Normally you do not need a flood risk assessment for a development on a site in flood zone 1.

You need a flood risk assessment is for all development in flood zone 1:

  • with a site area of 1 hectare or more (a hectare is about 2.5 acres)
  • in areas with critical drainage problems
  • where the local planning authority’s (LPA) strategic flood risk assessment (SFRA) shows it will be at increased flood risk in future
  • that increases its vulnerability classification because where the LPA’s SRFA shows it is at risk from other sources of flooding

If none of these categories apply, you do not need flood risk assessment data for your planning application.
