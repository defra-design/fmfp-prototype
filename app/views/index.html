February 2023

Release 2.5

This version concentrates on further optimising the results pages based UR sessions.

Version details

Changes

  • Refined content based on user feedback
  • Attempted to better associate the download of the PDF with them map
  • Moved the map information into a details component to remove some of the cognitive weight of the page
  • Moving to specifically asking users to draw a boundary, removing the marker choice and tidying up associated pages of the journey. This will hopefully make for a smoother journey at both ends of the process, with less work for the business and less hassle for the user
  • Removing references to the marker from pages, legends and ensuring journeys match up.
  • Starting to test changes in FZ1 results to be more focussed on downloading a P1 report and being stronger on the fact that an FRA is only needed in certain circumstances.
  • Moving the P1 Flood map for planning button to a more visible location and referencing it more specifically in the copy
  • Rewriting all the Flood Zone results pages to make them more logical, to make next steps clearer, and to reflect the move of the P1 download button
  • Adjustments to the design for 'Areas benefitting from defences' information due to changes in poilicy, including results, copy, map legend and more
  • Added a Flood Zone 2 results pages as there has never been one in the prototype before.
  • Updated the accessibility statement after issues were highligthed by the accessibility team.
  • Archive of the pages as they were tested for this version.
