Depending on the data that's available, your flood risk assessment data will include:

  • a flood zone map (flood map for planning)
  • historic flood outlines
  • relevant modelled flood levels and extents
  • flood defence locations and attributes

You also need to make a separate request a Flood Defence Breach Hazard Map from the Greater Manchester Merseyside and Cheshire Environment Agency team at Inforequests.GMMC@environment-agency.gov.uk. This will be available if modelling has been carried out for breach scenarios.

You should also check the risk from surface water and other sources of flooding when doing a flood risk asssessment.
