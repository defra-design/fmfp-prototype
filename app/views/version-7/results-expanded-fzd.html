Your results explained

Your development is in flood zone 3. It is also in an area that has some protection from flood defences.

Without the local flood defences, this area would be at high risk of flooding.

You'll need to do a flood risk assessment as part of your planning application.

Next steps

You should:

  1. speak to your local authority to find out more about their planning requirements - your local authority is Colchester Borough Council
  2. work with a flood risk consultant to get a flood risk assessment

Flood zones explained

Flood zones determine how likely it is that a location will flood.

All locations in flood zones 2 and 3 need a flood risk assessment. Some locations in flood zone 1 need a flood risk assessment.
