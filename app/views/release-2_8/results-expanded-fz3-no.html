Your results explained

Because your selected location is in flood zone 3, it has a high probability of flooding. This means you’ll need to do a flood risk assessment as part of your planning application.

See these results on a map

Map data

The flood map for planning shows river and sea flooding data only. It doesn’t include other sources of flooding. It is for use in development planning and flood risk assessments.

The data in the map relates to the selected location and is not specific to any property within it. The map is updated regularly and is correct at the time of publication.

Next steps

  1. Speak to your local authority to find out more about their planning requirements - your local authority is Warrington Borough Council.
  2. Work with a flood risk consultant to get a flood risk assessment.

Flood zones explained

Flood zones are based on how likely it is that a location will flood from rivers and the sea.

Flood zone 1

Locations have a low probability of flooding, some locations will need a flood risk assessment. Find out more about which developments need a flood risk assessment in flood zone 1.

Flood zone 2

Locations have a medium probability of flooding and will need a flood risk assessment.

Flood zone 3

Locations have a high probability of flooding and will need a flood risk assessment.

Getting a flood risk assessment

We recommend that you work with a flood risk consultant to get your flood risk assessment.

To request flood risk assessment data for this location, contact the Environment Agency team in Greater Manchester, Merseyside and Cheshire at Inforequests.GMMC@environment-agency.gov.uk

Your email should say that you are requesting flood risk assessment data (also known as a product 4) and include:

  • the address
  • a map showing the site boundary

More help and advice

For more advice around flood risk assessments and planning permission you could:

  • contact your local authority, Warrington Borough Council
  • read guidance on planning permission
  • contact the Environment Agency
